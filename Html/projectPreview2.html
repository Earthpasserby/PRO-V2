GIG Description

Introduction:

We are looking for a skilled UI/UX Designer to lead the redesign of our job platform, Singular. The project involves creating a payment portal that facilitates recurring payments to contractors using a structure that supports Agile Scrum methodologies. The platform redesign will be implemented using Flutter and Airtable. Deliverables: Design and develop a user-friendly payment portal interface.
