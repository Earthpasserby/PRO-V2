Banking savings app

2020

Adobe XD

UX Research

Figma

Project description

Banking savings app is an app that helps users save a little for every transaction they carry out, this also helps them ensure they are aware of the requirements.

Solution

We design a product that would ensure that users are able to save their funds directly from their account without any form of hassle while also investing in the process, this was a very strenuous process because users have a mind of their own with every new feature introduced into the project.

Your contribution

I was the lead product designer on the project and I focused more on ensuring that the product follows trough with the necessary requirements.

Key results

  • We were able to identify that 35% of the people who interacted with the product are students and working class.
  • The solution really took a whole new turn when we launched some of the features are rarely used.
  • 40% of new users joined the product for the first time.
